Some common music genres and their respective average BPMs...

- **House** - 118 - 135 BPM
- **Hip-Hop** - 80 - 115 BPM
- **UK Garage/2-step** - 130 - 135 BPM
- **Jungle/Drum n Bass** - 160 - 180 BPM
- **Acid Techno** - 135 - 150 BPM
- **Juke/Footwork** - 160 BPM
- **Trap** - 140 BPM
- **Triphop/Downtempo** - 90 - 110 BPM
